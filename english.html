Artist Statement

Devante Jordan, Paris Johnson, Daniel Hernandez, Charles Butts, and Atori Baker

Tranquil Wings
Cardboard, paint, and blinds
2015
In our art installation, Tranquil Wings, we wanted to create a 3 dimensional work that depicts peace and divinity. We cut out cardboard into shapes of wings, then used blinds and cut them out as feathers. We wanted to create an art installation that will make the school look better and give students and visitors a feeling of tranquility. When walking through the hallways and they see our installation, the dark/cool colors will help create a tranquil mood. The actual wings symbolize freedom and clarity of mind.
